Tea

Tea is the most widely consumed beverage in the world, second only to water. There are many varieties of Tea; Green Tea, Black Tea, Oolong Tea, White Tea and Puerh.

Coffee

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant. The two most commonly grown are the highly regarded arabica, and the less sophisticated but stronger and more hardy robusta.
